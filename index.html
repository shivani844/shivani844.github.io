About dorje.zen

As a trained quantitative economist and transaction manager in the renewable energy sector, Dorje started his own journey several years back when he noticed that a high-speed business life with little time left for himself or his loved ones did not make him happy. So, he began to deep-dive into personal growth literature, NLP (neuro-linguistic programming) training and the Wim Hof Method (breathwork, ice bathing, mindset training).

He travelled to Asia on a frequent basis to train martial arts, to study self-development techniques, meditation and breathwork, practice yoga, expand his mindset and spend lots of time in nature. His ongoing travels led him to return to Rishikesh in India where he studied Pranayama (traditional yogic breathing techniques) and meditation extensively until he was awarded his Meditation Teacher Training license (500 hours program).

Dorje also travelled to the Netherlands and Poland to join Wim Hof in extensive ice bathing and breathwork sessions.

Methods and Philosophy

By connecting the ancient with the modern we can use the advantages of both worlds to our benefit.

As an academic, Dorje was mainly interested in the neurological and behavioral patterns in economic decision making that cause financial crises. Later, he combined his scientific training background with insights from extensive travels to India where he traced back the roots of the many ancient wisdom teachings.

Breathwork: Breathing is one of the most powerful tools to biohack our operating system such that the desired mental and bio-energetic state can be reached. By breathing in a certain way, we can actively influence our nervous system, connect to our subconscious mind and control our stress levels. Learning how to breathe properly is one of the most empowering experiences everyone should make.

Meditation: Meditation, the art of emptying our minds, is a very effective technique which has been passed down from generation to generation for thousands of years. The resulting mental state creates great relaxation and serenity and provides a robust basis for living a fulfilled human life. Meditation is also a unique self-inquiry tool that helps us understand ourselves better which is the first step to making changes in our lives.

Holistic Health: Understanding our own biology and our innate responses to the ecosystem we live in is essential to playing our highest game. While our environment evolves rapidly, our brains and bodies have not changed much since stone age. Therefore, Dorje uniquely combines his biohacker’s toolbox with aspects of modern mental coaching and timeless spiritual wisdom to boost your physical as well as your mental and emotional health.
